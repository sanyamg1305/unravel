the foundation

Sukoon exists because sometimes people don't need someone to fix them.

They need a place where they can be honest without being judged.

What we believe

Healing isn't linear.
You deserve kindness, especially from yourself.
Every emotion matters, even the inconvenient ones.
Asking for help is strength, not failure.
No one should have to struggle alone.

The Privacy Promise

Everything you write here belongs to you. We do not sell your thoughts. We do not track your pain for advertising. What happens in the Attic stays in the Attic, encrypted before it ever leaves your device.

The Open Door

If this project speaks to you, there's room for you here too.

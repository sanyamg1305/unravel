the attic

This is your private space.

Everything you keep here is encrypted on your own device before it's ever saved. What happens in the Attic stays in the Attic.

The Attic needs a Supabase project to store your account and encrypted entries. Create a free one at supabase.com, run supabase/schema.sql in its SQL editor, then add your project URL and anon key to js/supabase-config.js.

This password also unlocks the encryption on your entries. If you forget it, we cannot recover what's saved, by design.
